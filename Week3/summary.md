Summary:
- 50% of users were able to complete the lesson.
    - That is lower than what we expected, expected >90%.
- 83% of users believed that the UI was appealing for daily use.
- 92% of users found it easy to select the language to learn, 8% of users said "maybe" suggesting it might me unclear for some users
- 100% of users suggested there was diversity and/or representation seen in the app.
- 85% of users stated it was clear when they answered a question correctly/incorrectly, 15% of users said "neutral".
- 38% of users stated that there was features missing from what they expected from the Duolingo App.
  - some users wrote about their opinion of the mascot, while others wrote about the speed/efficiency of the app.

- For future surveys:
    - Ask more specific questions
    - For more user-unique responses, ask more open-ended questions for qualitative results.
